Freqently Asked Questions

Main Page

This is where you can find most of the frequently asked questions



Question: Why does VR_Game not support Non-VR?

Answer: The game mechanics we plan on using only work in VR, like the menus and objects that have physics.



Question: How do I make an account?

Answer: It uses your steam account, no need to make an account.



Question: Is there Oculus Quest finger tracking support?

Answer: No, it is not currently on our list of things to do. But that doesn’t mean it will never be a feature.



Question: What VR headsets are supported?

Answer: Any headset capable of PCVR.



Question: Where can I buy the game?

Answer: When we release the game, we plan on only releasing on the Steam Store.



Question: When can I buy the game?

Answer: There isn't a planned release date yet.



Question: Will there be a Beta / Early Access period?

Answer: We haven't decided if we want to do that yet. But it is a possibility
